Start your campaign

1%Club is the go-to crowdfunding platform for doing good. We empower people with great ideas to create positive change in their community, city, country – or in the world. On the 1%Club website you can kick-start initiatives and raise funds via your own network using our innovative tools. We aim to make crowdfunding the most simple and rewarding experience for everyone who wants to do good.

    What we expect from your campaign:
  • Your campaign contributes to a better world.
  • You can show how the money will be spent with an itemised budget.
Start your campaign now
What you need to know before starting:
How crowdfunding works on 1%Club Crowdfunding is all about activating your own network. Therefore it’s important that you are able to inspire and activate people around you to support your campaign. Be prepared to be very busy for 100 days!

When you start your campaign you can fill in all the details about your crowdfunding campaign. You can save this at any point to continue working on this later. After you submit your plan, we will review it and keep you updated all along the way. As soon your plan is approved you can start crowdfunding!
More tips in our FAQ
Some of our most important rules
  • The online campaign can last up to 100 days.
  • You choose your own target amount.
  • Crowdfunded more than 100%? You can raise even more funds!
  • If you reach your target, our service fee is 7%.
  • If, unfortunately, you don’t, this will be 12%.
